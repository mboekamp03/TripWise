For Phone Device

1 / 8
Create an account on the sign-up screen by entering your email and creating a password. When you sign up, you will receive an email to confirm your account. If you already have an account, use your credentials to sign in on the sign-in page instead.
2 / 8
On this screen, you can choose to either skip straight to the list of destinations we provide to explore, or you can choose to take the quiz to determine which destination you should travel to based on your wants.
3 / 8
If you choose to take the quiz, this is the screen you will be taken to. Work through the questions thoughtfully. When you are done, click the blue submit button.
4 / 8
If you choose to skip straight to the destination list, this is the screen you will be taken to. To view a destination, simply click on the place you would like to explore.
5 / 8
Before you are taken to the destination screen you chose, you will be prompted to input the start and end dates of your trip, so that the available lodging will be for your specific dates.
6 / 8
When you've made it to the destination screen, you will be met with pictures and a description of your chosen destination. To be taken to the available places to stay, click the "See Available Lodging" button. To receive a personalized travel plan generated by ChatGPT, click the "See Travel Plan" button. To see available flights to your destination, click the "See Flights" button.
7 / 8
If you choose to see available, you will then be prompted to choose to look at either hotels or airbnbs for the area. Choose one to browse.
8 / 8
This is an example of the hotel page. Click on a hotel to navigate to the website to book!
